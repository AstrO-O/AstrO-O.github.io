Marshmello

Christopher Comstock,known by his stage name Marshmello,is an American electronic music DJ and producer. The DJ first shot to fame with his popular remixes of songs by American DH duo Jack U and Russian-German DJ Zedd. Some of his most popular songs include "Silence", "Wolves","Friends","Happier", and "Alone" all of which have since been certified multi-platinum and topped the charts.
